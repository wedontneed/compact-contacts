Quick help

Contacts do not appear: Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts and allow Compact Contacts, then return to the app.

A contact cannot be edited: Some directory, Exchange, CardDAV, or linked records may be read-only. Compact Contacts shows editing only when the owning account exposes a writable record.

Purchase or feature access: Compact Contacts is a paid app. Every Standard feature is available immediately after download; there is no trial, subscription, in-app purchase, restore screen, or Pro tier.

Duplicate contacts: Choose Contact → Find Potential Duplicates… and review the possible matches, matching reasons, and account ownership. This review is read-only; use Open Apple Contacts if you decide to edit, link, merge, or delete records.

Import or export: vCard operations always use a macOS open/save panel. Compact Contacts never uploads the selected file.

Diagnostics

Settings → Advanced → Export Redacted Diagnostics… creates a redacted JSON report on your Mac. It is designed to exclude contact names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, notes, search text, and contact identifiers. Review any file before sharing it.

Contact support

Email wedontneed@gmail.com. Include your macOS version, Compact Contacts version, the account type involved, and steps to reproduce. Do not send an address-book export or real contact data.
